Instructions

  1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. Player X always goes first.
  3. Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in an empty square.
  4. The first player to get three of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins.
  5. If all 9 squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
